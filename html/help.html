How to Play

Rules

1. The goal of blackjack is simple - to get as close as possible to 21 without going over, and to have a higher hand than the dealer. If the dealer goes over the score of 21, they bust and lose the game. The same goes for you.

2. Your Move: Decide whether to 'Hit' or 'Stand'.

3. Choose to 'Hit': Ask the dealer for another card. Do this when, based on the current value of your cards, either you're sure that the next card will not cause you going bust, or you're willing to run the risk of getting a better hand than the dealer. |OR| Choose to 'Stand': Ask the dealer to move on and deal you no more cards. You'll probably do this when the value of your cards is quite high already (e.g. 17 or greater)

4. As a result of the move you just made, you will probably have a new hand value. You'll remain in the game if your hand is valued at 21 or anything less.

5. After you have completed your move, the dealer will reveal their hand.

6. If your hand is closer to 21 than that of the dealer, you bust the dealer and win OR If the dealer has 21 or a closer score to 21 than you, the dealer wins.

Scoring System

Plus Points

For every hand draw you get 5 points.

For every hand win you score 10 x the difference between hands.

If player2 gets busted you get 20 points.

Losing Points

For every hand lost you lose 5 points.

If you get busted 10 points get deducted from your score.
